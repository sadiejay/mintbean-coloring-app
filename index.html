About Colurs

Colurs is a FREE online, open source, and easy to use coloring tool. What does all this mean? No system requirements! Our program can be ran on any web browser. We wanted to make Drawing and Coloring digitally accessible for everyone. Are you a developer? Our Open Source code allows any lover of our app to add features or update it. Rather you use our app for relaxing or to practice drawing we hope that our app brings a smile to your face and a new appreciation for art!
